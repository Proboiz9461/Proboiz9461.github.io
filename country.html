👻

Phantom Load

Devices plugged in but off silently drain power. A single 10W charger left plugged in wastes ~87 kWh/year — enough to power an LED bulb for 8,700 hours.

📺

TV Standby

India has ~200 million TVs. Each wastes ~40 kWh/year on standby. Nationally that's 8 TWh/year — enough to power Goa for 2 years.

🔌

30% Home Waste

Up to 30% of a typical Indian household's electricity is wasted — from standby power, old appliances, and faulty wiring. Idle standby alone is ~5–10%.
